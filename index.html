Introduction to Tape

Do you have boring pens and want something as pretty as a flower? I can help you there by teaching you how to make a duct tape flower pen.

We will start by making something easy, like a flower. For this you will need 4 things duct tape, scissors, a straw or pen, and a ruler.

Steps

Cover the pen or straw with tape:
  • Cut 15 2" pieces of tape.
  • Take 2" pieces and trun it sticky side up.
  • Take top right corner and fold it in well leaving a tiny bit of sticky at the bottom and then do the same thing on the left side and overlap the left side to the right.
  • Once you fold all 15 piece take them one by one and stick them to the staw or pen and rap each of them arond.
