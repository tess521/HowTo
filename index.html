Sticky Situation

Do you have boring pens and want something as pretty as a flower? I can help you there by teaching you how to make a duct tape flower pen.

We will start by making something easy, like a flower. For this you will need 4 things duct tape, scissors, a straw or pen, and a ruler.

Steps

1. Cover the pen or straw with tape. 2. Cut 15 2" pieces of tape. 3. Take 2" pieces and trun it sticky side up. 4. Take top right corner and fold it in well leaving a tiny bit of sticky at the bottom and then do the same thing on the left side and overlap the left side to the right. 5. Once you fold all 15 piece take them one by one and stick them to the staw or pen and rap each of them arond. .
